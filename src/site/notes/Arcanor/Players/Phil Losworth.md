# [[Arcanor/Players/Phil Losworth\|Phil Losworth]]

Has a dog Rupert

Urban bounty hunter from Othengrad. Most of his life he was completing bounty contracts and minding his own business. At some point he felt a lack of purpose that keeps him up at night. People around him die from famine and sickness, asking for help, asking for someone to act. But who can do something about this?

In the last 3 towns he visited, he came across some contracts posted. 
They all say roughly the same thing. *‘Missing Persons: Reward Offered.’, ‘Disappeared: Last Seen in Dragonspine.’*. Some common folk even suggested that *‘Sewer folk taking people for food.',* or that  *'it's the works of the devil.',* even that *'Cults sacrifice humans to gods in exchange for food'*.
While not his bag of beans, it seems like something that he could do. Utilising his knowledge in finding people, maybe he can help some poor soul along the way.

As he was journeying towards [[Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Othengrad/Dragonspine/Dragonspine\|Dragonspine]], at a tavern ([[Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Othengrad/Τρελό Κοτέτσι\|Τρελό Κοτέτσι]]) outside [[Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Othengrad/Draumfell\|Draumfell]], he met 3 adventurers that wanted to travel to [[Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Helm's Reach/Helm's Reach\|Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Helm's Reach/Helm's Reach]] as quick as possible. He proposed to them, to travel together through [[Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Othengrad/Mt. Everos\|Mt. Everos]], a dangerous path that would cut down the trip time in half. They would all reach [[Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Othengrad/Dragonspine/Dragonspine\|Dragonspine]] faster and safer. Win win for everyone.

## Background

Philip never knew his parents. He was told they’d died soon after his birth at the [[Mercy’s Hands\|Mercy’s Hands]] orphanage in [[Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Othengrad/Draumfell\|Draumfell]], where he was raised in an actually decent manner. The orphanage was a good place, funded in part by the church of Aeloria, and the nurses and staff there took pride in raising lost children into upstanding citizens.

Philip, along with other physically skilled children were often sent to train with the city guard, and eventually conscripted at 17. The surname Lawsworth was given to him on his “graduation” from the orphanage as a reminder of the values he was meant to uphold. 

He stayed in the corps for 8 years before the rigidity of the structures and the questionable “whys” of his assignments chafed too much to bear. Bounty hunting was the obvious alternative, and the one he took. It was around 3 years into this line of work he started signing as “Losworth” instead — a personal reminder of what his tutors hoped for him, and what he did with that hope.

Nevertheless, he was good. Quick and efficient, not too boasty- practical. He moved around a lot, floating across [[Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Othengrad/Othengrad\|Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Othengrad/Othengrad]] whenever local gangs started getting too much for him, and a lot of time passed this way.

Phil is now 41, graying and balding. He keeps a dusty room in [[Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Othengrad/Draumfell\|Draumfell]], where he sleeps less than half the time, still travelling around on jobs. His marriage didn’t work out because he was never around, but at least he has left no kids behind. [[Martha\|Martha]] doesn’t hate him, though it would be nice if she did, ‘cause her disappointment stings too-familiar. He’s made more money than he needs, so he gives a lot back to the orphanage where he was raised. His name doesn’t show up in any of the donor lists, of course — which one would he sign with? Too embarrassing. He also volunteers there when he’s in town, teaching fencing or helping clean up. He likes the kids, even though they keep asking awkward questions about his scars. They make him feel… decent. 

At 39, he promised to himself he’d turn his life around. He hasn’t.
