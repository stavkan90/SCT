# [[Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Helm's Reach/Caedun\|Caedun]]

The old capital or helm's reach, now stands as a necropolis, a grim testament to [[Arcanor/Deities/Mortuumar\|Mortuumar]]’s power. No living soul dares to enter its haunted streets, where the undead reign supreme and the air is thick with the stench of death. Caedun’s ruins are a place of nightmares, its once-proud structures now crumbling and overrun by the damned.
