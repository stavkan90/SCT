# [[Arcanor/Magic Items/Umbrafang (Cursed)\|Umbrafang (Cursed)]]

_Wondrous Item, Requires Attunement_
_The sword description._

While attuned to the sword you gain the following benefits:

- **Aspirant (Level 9)**
    - +1 bonus to AC, Saving Throws.
    - Your Constitution score becomes 19. You can use your Constitution for attack and damage rolls.
    - You can switch this weapon between melee and ranged (30ft/60ft range).
- **Adept (Level 13)**
    - When you hit a creature, you can spend Hit Dice to deal extra necrotic damage equal to the total rolled. This effect can't be invoked again until the next short rest.
- **Master (Level 17)**
    - 10 charges per long rest.
        - When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can expend a charge to deal an extra 4d6 necrotic damage to the target, and you regain a number of hit points equal to the necrotic damage dealt.
        - When a creature you can see damages you, you can expend a charge and use your reaction to make a melee attack against that creature, with advantage on your attack roll.
