[[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Players/Slyrak\|Slyrak]] was born a human child in the peaceful village of [[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Glimmerwald/Wyndhaven\|Wyndhaven]] of [[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Glimmerwald/Glimmerwald\|ttrpgs/Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Glimmerwald/Glimmerwald]], where his parents lived as humble farmers. His life was simple and full of love until the calamity of [[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Deities/Mortuumar\|Mortuumar]] descended upon his home when he was just eight years old. The dark forces overran Wyndhaven, bringing death and corruption. In the chaos, [[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Players/Slyrak\|Slyrak]]'s parents urged him to flee, sacrificing themselves so he might survive. 
Terrified and heartbroken, [[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Players/Slyrak\|Slyrak]] ran into the wilderness, escaping the devastation that claimed his village. Lost and alone in the dense forest, he wandered aimlessly, battling hunger and fear. One night, as he lay under the stars, a strange and powerful transformation overcame him. His body was infused with an ancient magic that had lain dormant within him, turning him into an Aasimar. This innate magic, awakened by the dire circumstances of his escape, marked him as something far beyond human, a rare gift that set him apart from all others. 
[[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Players/Slyrak\|Slyrak]]’s journey took a darker turn when a group of criminals found him in the wild. Recognizing his potential, they sold him to a wealthy family in the great [[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Nah/City of Nah\|City of Nah]]. 
This family, eager to gain prestige and power, saw [[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Players/Slyrak\|Slyrak]] as the perfect candidate to be trained as a Paladin. In this world, having a Paladin in the family was the highest honor, a symbol of strength. Raised in luxury but treated more as a possession than a son, [[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Players/Slyrak\|Slyrak]] was trained relentlessly in the ways of combat, discipline, and honor. 
Though his life was comfortable, he never forgot the loss of his parents or the horrors of [[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Deities/Mortuumar\|Mortuumar]]. As he honed his skills, he vowed to use his newfound power to protect others from the same fate that had befallen his family and to honor the memory of his lost home, [[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Regions of Arcanor/Glimmerwald/Wyndhaven\|Wyndhaven]].
