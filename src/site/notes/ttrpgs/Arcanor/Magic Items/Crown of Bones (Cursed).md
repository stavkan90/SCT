# [[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Magic Items/Crown of Bones (Cursed)\|Crown of Bones (Cursed)]]

_Wondrous Item, Requires Attunement_
_The crown is made of rusty silver._

While attuned to the crown you gain the following benefits:

**Aspirant (Level 9)**
    - You always know which way is North and have a perfect memory of paths you have traveled.
    - You can breathe in any environment and are immune to gases and vapors.
- **Adept (Level 13)**
    - While wearing no armor, your AC is 18.
    - Creatures have Disadvantage on attack rolls against you.
- **Master (Level 17)**
    - As an action, you can become Invisible. This invisibility does not end if you attack, cast a spell, or use a class feature.

---
## The Dark Gift (The Curse)
*The crown is not worn on the skull; it is part of it.*

| **Tier** | **Curse Description**                                                                                                                    |
| :------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Aspirant | You register as an Abberation to spells and abilities (like *Divine Sense*), and you gain **Vulnerability to Radiant damage**.           |
| Adept    | • The crown merges with your skull. The flesh around your brow recedes and blackens, exposing the bone beneath where the crown attaches. |
