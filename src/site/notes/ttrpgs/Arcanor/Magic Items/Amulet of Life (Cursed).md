# [[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Magic Items/Amulet of Life (Cursed)\|Amulet of Life (Cursed)]]

_Wondrous Item, Requires Attunement_
_The amulet is made of vines that hold a red gem. While wearing it, vines tend to go underneath a person's skin._

While attuned to the amulet you gain the following benefits:

**Aspirant (Level 9)**
    - +1 bonus to AC and Saving Throws.
    - your Constitution score becomes 19.
- **Adept (Level 13)**
    - your Strength score becomes 25.
    - +2 to attack/damage rolls.
- **Master (Level 17)**
    - +3 to attack/damage rolls.
    - The first time you attack on each of your turns, you can transfer all or part of the weapon's +3 bonus to your Armor Class. The chosen bonus applies to your AC until the start of your next turn.

---
## The Curse
*The vines don't just mimic life; they replace it.*

| **Tier** | **Curse Description**                                                     |
| :------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Aspirant | Your skin is highly flammable. You gain **Vulnerability to Fire damage**. |
| Adept    | Blackened, thorny vines burrow beneath your skin, replacing your veins    |
