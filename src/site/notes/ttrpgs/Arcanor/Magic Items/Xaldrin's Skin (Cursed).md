# [[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Magic Items/Xaldrin's Skin (Cursed)\|Xaldrin's Skin (Cursed)]]

_Wondrous Item, Requires Attunement_
_The mask is a curved piece of wood. When attached to a creature's face, it becomes a full face mask. How it looks might change depending on the creature that wears it._

While attuned to the mask you gain the following benefits:

- **Aspirant (Level 9)**
    - You can cast *Disguise Self* at will
    - 30 feet from you, each subject who is sleeping naturally when combat begins, magically awakens and has advantage on its initiative rolls. This benefit doesn't wake a subject from magically induced sleep.
- **Adept (Level 13)**
    - Your movement is unaffected by difficult terrain, and magical effects can neither reduce your speed nor cause you to be paralyzed or restrained.
    - 6 charges per long rest. Use a charge to cast the effects of a haste spell.
- **Master (Level 17)**
    - 3 charges per long rest. Take a Magic action and expend 1 charge to give yourself the invisible condition for 1 hour. The effect ends early if you decide it (no action required) or if you cease wearing the mask.

---
## The Curse
*The mask doesn't hide your face; it erases it.*

| Tier     | **Curse Description**                                                                                     |
| :------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Aspirant | If you remove the mask, your face is completely blank and smooth (no eyes, nose, or mouth)                |
| Adept    | • Because you have lost your true expressions, you have **Disadvantage on Charisma (Persuasion)** checks. |
