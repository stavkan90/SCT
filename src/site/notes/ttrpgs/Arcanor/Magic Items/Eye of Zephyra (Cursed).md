# [[ttrpgs/Arcanor/Magic Items/Eye of Zephyra (Cursed)\|Eye of Zephyra (Cursed)]]

_Wondrous Item , Requires Attunement_
_The sapphire is cold and oily. Looking into it feels like falling into a storm._

While attuned to the eye you gain the following benefits:

- **Aspirant (Level 9)**
    - You can cast *Guidance* at will.
    - 10 charges per long rest. You can use an action to cast: *Augury* (2), *Detect Thoughts* (2), *Clairvoyance* (3), *Divination* (4), or *Scrying* (5).
- **Adept (Level 13)**
    - **+1** bonus to attack and damage rolls. When you hit a non-humanoid creature, it takes an extra **1d10** psychic damage.
    - You and all allies within **10** feet of you have Advantage on Saving Throws against spells and other magical effects.
- **Master (Level 17)**
    - **+2** bonus to attack and damage rolls. When you hit a non-humanoid creature, it takes an extra **2d10** psychic damage.
    - You and all allies within **30** feet of you have Advantage on Saving Throws against spells and other magical effects.
    - As a reaction when an ally you can see fails a saving throw, you can force them to reroll. They must use the new roll.

## The Curse
*To see the future is to forget how to live in the present.*

| **Tier** | **Curse**                                                                                                                                                |
| :------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Aspirant | You have disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks. The overlap of "what is" and "what might be" makes the present moment look like a smudged painting. |
| Adept    | Your eyes become black orbs filled with swirling gray mist, just like the eye of zephyra.                                                                |
